The Bachelor Cookbook

Resource-based recipes for the ill-prepared chef

For single men living alone, dinnertime is a necessity, not an occasion. We don't have pantries full of sundries or a crisper full of green things; we have objects in our possession that are either edible, spoiled, or unknown.

Doesn't matter if it's dinosaur-shaped pasta, tiny onions in a jar, or half a pack of hot dogs, we're going to eat as fast as possible and get on with our lives. There are horses to be ridden and steel to be forged and we cannot be bothered with paltry nutritional dilemmas.
