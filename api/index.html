The Bachelor Cookbook

Resource-based recipes for the ill-prepared chef

Real men don't have time to "grocery shop", we're too busy riding horses and forging steel. Sometimes there's no crisper full of green things or cedar shelf lined with baby-sized jars of fancy-pants spices. Usually you're stuck with condiments and various unperishables, like dry pasta, tiny onions in a jar, SPAM if you're lucky. We make the best of what we've got, and dammit, it's delicious.
