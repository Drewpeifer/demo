The Bachelor Cookbook

Resource-based recipes for the ill-prepared chef

For the modern bachelor, dinnertime is a necessity, not an occasion. We don't have pantries full of sundries or a crisper full of greens; we have objects in our possession that are either edible, spoiled, or unknown. Somehow, they must become sustenance.

Search below for recipes containing whatever pathetic ingredients you currently possess, and marvel at the edible wonders you can create.
